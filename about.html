About Industrial Control Ireland

Industrial Control Ireland also ICI, is based in Middleton, county Cork. We offer Electrical services nationwide.
Here at ICI we specialise in maintenance, troubleshooting and installation of a wide range of automated and industrial electrical systems. These include but are not limited to Siemens, Allen Bradley, ESA and ABB to name a few. Experience with ATEX directives combined with automated systems makes safety and efficiency the main goals that we strive to achieve. Industrial Control Ireland can cost-effectively integrate automation into your manufacturing processes to help you increase

  • Efficiency,
  • Improve production rates,
  • Attain higher quality levels,
  • Ensure high safety levels.
